2023 Constructors' Championship

Each constructor has two drivers racing over the year to win points for their team and win the coveted constructors' championship. The championship is fiercely competitive, as the performance of both cars is vital to a team's success, ensuring that every driver plays a pivotal role in achieving the team's goals.

The 2023 Constructors' Championship was a thrilling contest. While Red Bull dominated, Mercedes, and Ferrari battled hard for second place. Teams like Aston Martin and McLaren, also showcased remarkable progress, fighting tirelessly to be at the top of the mid-table so they can give the big 3 a run for their money in the 2024 season.
